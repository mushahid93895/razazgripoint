The Man Behind It All

Hello! Main Mushahid Raza hoon. Ek middle-class parivar se aane ke bawajood, mere ghar walon ne hamesha mere har sapne ko poora support kiya hai. Unka yahi support meri sabse badi taqat hai.

Bachpan se mera ek hi khwab tha—ek Cricketer banna. 12th class mein maine apne trials diye aur State Level tak khela bhi, lekin zindagi ne kuch aisi karwat li ki mujhe cricket ko alvida kehna pada. Padhai mein main shuru se hi acha tha; 10th mein maine 81% score kiya jisme Maths mein mere 91 marks the. Halanki, aage chalkar maine apna rasta badla aur 11th se Agriculture ko chun liya. 12th mein 84% marks laane ke baad, aaj main BSc Agriculture (Hons.) ke 2nd semester ka student hoon.

Kheti aur padhai ke sath-sath, mujhe computers ki bahut deep knowledge hai. Maine ADCP (Advanced Diploma In Computer Programming) kiya hua hai. Yahi wajah hai ki jab maine agri-students ko notes ke liye pareshan hote dekha, toh maine apne is tech knowledge ka use karke Razaagripoint bana diya!
